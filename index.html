Hello, I am Liam Alexander Quinn, a graphic designer based in Naarm/Melbourne. I work at Weekdays, a design and branding studio, working with a bunch of cool hospitality businesses. I'm also head designer at Veraison, a formerly physical wine zine which is currently transitioning to a website. Before Weekdays, I was at Blackhearts & Sparrows as their in-house designer; working on campaigns, brand work, product designs, packaging, signage, and everything in between.

Recently, most of my studio work has been designing and developing websites, motion graphics and video work.
